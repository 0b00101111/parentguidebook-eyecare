## A Note on How We Use Sources

We do not reproduce copyrighted text. All articles on this site are written in our own words based on the information and findings in the sources listed above. Where we cite specific statistics, study results, or clinical recommendations, we indicate the source in the article text using bracketed references (e.g., [Source: src1]) that correspond to the numbered sources at the bottom of each article.

We encourage you to read the original sources whenever possible. Many are freely available online through the links provided above.

If you believe we have misrepresented any source or made a factual error, please contact us at **eyecare@parentguidebook.org** and we will review and correct it promptly.
